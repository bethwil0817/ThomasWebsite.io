My name is Thomas Dijkstra. I've been involved in the music scene since I could ride my bike to the local music store. Since then I've been fully immersed in music from working at Island Music for 5 year's, running sound, working at open mics, playing lead guitar, singing for various bands, and always playing as much as I possibly can.

I've been so lucky to play all over Wisconsin and open for artists I've always looked up to.

Folk and outlaw county have always been my main focus and what you can expect if you see me out playing!

LIVE SHOWS
My live music style incorporates covers and original music. Genres include outlaw country, folk, indie, and blues rock, with similar sounds to Matt Mason and Colter Wall. Check out my Youtube page for more live recordings of my music!
